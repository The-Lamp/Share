Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection

This website does not collect any personal information from users directly. No name, email address, or other identifiable data is stored or required from users visiting our site.

2. Third-Party Links

There are no third-party links on this website. All content is directly served by the site itself.

3. Analytics

We utilize a basic analytics system limited to ad interaction insights. This data is collected only to improve ad targeting and understand engagement with specific advertisements. However, no IP address, geographic location, or other personally identifiable information is stored or analyzed as part of this system.

4. User Security and Privacy

Our website maintains high standards of user privacy and does not store or track any user behavior outside of ad interaction analytics. There is no form or login system on this site.
